# “Analysis on Pizza Place Sales Data”

### Task Overview

This project analyzes a year’s worth of sales data from a fictional pizza restaurant his project analyzes a year’s worth of sales data from a fictitious pizza place, including the date and time of each order and the pizzas served, with additional details such as pizza type, size, quantity, price, and ingredients. 
The object is to understand:
- Overall sales performance- Revenue trends over time- Customer ordering patterns by day, month, and hour- Identify the best and worst-selling pizzas.
- Insights to help improve menu, pricing, and promotions
The analysis combines four CSV files — Orders, Order Details, Pizzas, and Pizza Types — into a single dataset to make analysis easier.
### Data Preparation
- The datasets were merged to include pizza name, size, quantity, price, revenue, and order date.
- Dates were converted to a datetime format for easy analysis by month, day, and hour.
  #### key Analysis & insightt

The pizza place generated strong annual revenue, selling nearly fifty thousand pizzas across over **twenty-one thousand customer orders** with a with **32 pizza types**, the restaurant offers strong menu variety, giving customers multiple choices and potentially attracting different preferences..The average pizza price of $16.46 suggests a balanced pricing strategy that supports both customer affordability and revenue growth.
Observing the peak hour of sales is **12 PM (12:00 hours)**:Sales are highest during lunchtime, showing strong lunchtime demand. This highlights the importance of proper staffing and potential lunch promotions during this period.
From the analysis, **Friday** records the highest sales.Customers tend to purchase more pizzas toward the end of the week. This may be due to weekend preparation, social gatherings, or reduced home cooking on Fridays. Promotions or special deals on Fridays could further increase revenue.
From the anaysis The top 5 bestselling pizzas (based on quantity sold) are:
The Classic Deluxe Pizza,The Barbecue Chicken Pizza,The Hawaiian Pizza,The Pepperoni Pizza, The Thai Chicken Pizza.
From the line chart showing total monthly sales, the following pattern is observed:
-Sales increase steadily from January and reach the highest point in July.After July, sales begin to decline.A noticeable drop occurs in September and October.There is a slight recovery in November.
Sales are generally higher in the middle of the year and lower toward the end. This indicates a mid-year peak in demand. 
Some pizza types record significantly lower sales compared to others.
Overall, the exploratory analysis provide meaningful insight
